Welcome to this amazing API

  1. To get the complete list of recipes, make a GET request to the URL/recipes.
  2. To get the details of your desired recipe, make a GET request to the URL/recipes/dish-name. Replace dish-name with the name of the desired dish : Ponyo Ramen, Totoro Onigiri, Spirited Away Gyoza, Howl's Moving Castle Cake, Spirited Away Bento, Princess Mononoke Bowl.
  3. You will receive the details of the corresponding dish as a JSON response.
